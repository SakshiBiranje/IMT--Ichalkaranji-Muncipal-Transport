About IMT - Ichalkaranji Muncipal Transport

Revolutionizing urban mobility for the citizens of Ichalkaranji

Our Mission

The Ichalkaranji Muncipal Transport (IMT) is dedicated to transforming public transportation in Ichalkaranji by providing a modern, efficient, and user-friendly platform that connects citizens to the city's bus network.

We aim to reduce waiting times, simplify the booking process, and make public transportation accessible to all citizens through digital innovation.

What We Offer

Easy Route Search

Quickly find buses by simply entering your source and destination points. Get instant information on available routes, timings, and fares.

Online Booking

Book your bus tickets in advance from the comfort of your home. No more waiting in queues or rushing to catch a bus.

Digital Bus Passes

Apply for monthly bus passes online with secure Aadhaar verification. Receive digital passes directly on your mobile.

Real-time Tracking

Track your bus in real-time and know exactly when it will arrive. Plan your journey better with accurate ETAs.

Mobile OTP Verification

Secure booking and pass issuance with mobile OTP verification. Your data and transactions are always protected.

Booking History

Keep track of all your previous journeys and passes. Easy access to digital receipts and pass renewals.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a bus ticket online?

You can book a bus ticket by going to the Search page, entering your source and destination, selecting your preferred bus, and completing the booking process with payment.

What documents are needed for a bus pass?

For a bus pass, you need to provide your Aadhaar card and category-specific documents (student ID for student pass, age proof for senior citizen pass, etc.).

How long does it take to process a bus pass application?

Bus pass applications are usually processed within 48 hours after submission and verification. You will receive an SMS notification once your pass is approved.

Can I cancel my bus ticket booking?

Yes, you can cancel your bus ticket up to 1 hour before the scheduled departure time. The refund amount will depend on the cancellation policy.

Is the real-time tracking feature available for all buses?

Currently, real-time tracking is available for major routes within the city. We are working on expanding this feature to all routes in the near future.
