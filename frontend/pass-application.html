Monthly Bus Pass Application

Apply for a monthly bus pass with unlimited rides across Ichalkaranji city

Pass Information

Student Pass

₹300/month
  • Unlimited rides in city limits
  • Valid ID card required
  • 50% discount on regular pass

Senior Citizen Pass

₹350/month
  • Unlimited rides in city limits
  • Age proof required (60+ years)
  • 40% discount on regular pass

Regular Pass

₹600/month
  • Unlimited rides in city limits
  • Valid for 30 days from issue
  • Available for all citizens

Ladies Special Pass

₹400/month
  • Unlimited rides in city limits
  • Valid ID proof required
  • 33% discount on regular pass

Important Instructions

  • Pass will be issued after verification of your documents
  • Aadhaar verification is mandatory for all pass types
  • Category-specific documents are required (student ID, age proof, etc.)
  • Processing may take up to 48 hours after submission
  • Digital pass will be sent to your registered mobile number
